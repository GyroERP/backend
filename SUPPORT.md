## Community help

| Channel | Use for |
|---------|---------|
| [GitHub Issues](https://github.com/GyroERP/backend/issues) | Bug reports and feature requests |
| [GitHub Discussions](https://github.com/GyroERP/backend/discussions) | Questions, ideas, and general help |

Please search existing issues before opening a new one.

## What not to use public channels for

- **Security vulnerabilities** — email security@gyroerp.com (see [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md))
- **Commercial licensing** — email licensing@gyroerp.com
- **Legal or trademark matters** — email legal@gyroerp.com
- **Production data or secrets** — never paste credentials, customer data, or `.env` contents in issues

## Commercial support

Enterprise licensing, SaaS deployment, and commercial use require a separate
agreement. Contact **licensing@gyroerp.com**.
